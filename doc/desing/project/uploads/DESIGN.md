# Design System Strategy

## Vision & Identity
This design system is built on a foundation of professional reliability and modern geometric clarity. It balances a high-performance corporate aesthetic with contemporary typographic touches to ensure readability and trust.

## Typography
The system has transitioned to **Inter** across all text roles (Headlines, Body, and Labels). 
- **Typeface:** Inter
- **Character:** This font choice provides a highly functional, clean, and neutral aesthetic. It is engineered for maximum legibility on digital screens, ensuring that information is conveyed clearly without unnecessary ornamentation. It maintains a professional tone that is well-suited for both complex interfaces and long-form content.

## Color Palette
The color strategy utilizes a high-fidelity approach centered around a core brand blue.
- **Primary (#0052CC):** A deep, trustworthy blue used for primary actions and brand presence.
- **Tertiary (#D73A49):** A sophisticated crimson used for highlights, accents, and critical feedback loops.
- **Neutral (#F8F9FA):** A clean, cool-toned gray base for surfaces and backgrounds to ensure a crisp, light-mode experience.

## Layout & Shape
- **Roundedness (1):** UI components feature subtle rounding. This creates a precise, professional look that avoids the austerity of sharp corners while maintaining a serious, structured tone.
- **Spacing (2):** Internal and external margins follow a "Normal" density scale. This provides a balanced information density suitable for both analytical tools and standard web applications.

## Component Principles
- **Clarity:** Every element must be clearly defined against the neutral background.
- **Intent:** Use the primary blue to guide users toward the most important tasks.
- **Precision:** The subtle corner radius reflects a commitment to detail and structural integrity.
